# Product Direction

Pickaxe Capital should become a personal AI market intelligence operating system.

## Primary Pages

- Command Center: live watchlist, synchronized selected ticker, flow proxy, desk brief, journal
- Signals: option ideas with exact contracts, alert blueprints, risk gates, and Webull-only execution
- Agent Operations: living AI Habitat OS showing agents working together
- Checklist: auto-updating next actions
- Vision Map: long-term system map, business ecosystem, memory, quote, and catalyst notes
- A-Z Build: what has been built and why

## What Good Looks Like

- The user can click a ticker and the entire desk shifts focus.
- Agents visibly work, collaborate, and create activity logs.
- Signals read like trade blueprints, not vague alerts.
- The checklist tells the user exactly what to do next.
- The UI feels like a premium hedge fund command room from the future.
- Internally, the implementation stays small and understandable.

## Avoid

- Generic SaaS dashboards
- Marketing landing pages
- Automatic broker execution
- Huge abstractions before real data needs them
- Fake certainty in trading language
- Visual clutter that makes the desk slower
