# Pickaxe Capital Master Context

Read this before making architectural, UI, or engineering decisions.

Pickaxe Capital is a synchronized AI operating system for market intelligence, options research, business building, and long-term self-improvement. It is not a collection of widgets. Every page should feel connected to one living command center that helps the user move toward the April 18 2028 goal.

## North Star

Build a personal AI command system that produces trading signals, option ideas, research briefs, checklist actions, and learning loops while the user keeps execution manual in Webull.

The system should optimize for:

- interconnected intelligence
- synchronized market awareness
- premium futuristic UX
- simple internal architecture
- immersive external behavior
- living-system feedback loops
- billionaire-grade aesthetics
- risk-first options discipline

## Product Identity

- Company: Pickaxe Capital
- Operating vision: AI Habitat OS / TTT command center
- User role: CEO B, high-level allocator and final decision maker
- Trading mode: signals and alerts only; no broker execution
- Execution venue: Webull
- Current AI note: The OpenAI API key is still quota/billing-limited, so the signal engine is currently rules/data-based. Once billing/quota is active, the AI research layer can synthesize deeper reasoning on top of these signals.

## Core Philosophy

The site should feel alive, reactive, and strategically valuable. It should show agents working, data flowing, and decisions becoming memory.

The best version is calm under pressure: cinematic, premium, useful, and disciplined. It should avoid hype, clutter, fake precision, and overbuilt abstractions.

## Trading Philosophy

The trading system exists to find high-quality ideas, not force trades.

Every option idea must include:

- ticker and exact contract to inspect in Webull
- direction and thesis
- entry trigger
- invalidation
- target plan
- liquidity and spread awareness
- risk gate
- journal/learning loop

The system must constantly remind the user that max loss is premium paid and the website never places trades.

## TTT

TTT means Time, Trend, and Theme.

- Time: session timing, catalyst timing, expiration, patience
- Trend: price structure, levels, momentum, relative strength
- Theme: macro narrative, news, policy, sector flow, public attention

Quote: "Be sure to put your feet in the right place, then STAND FIRM." — TTT

## Catalyst Memory

Track unusual public-market catalysts, but treat them as context, not blind signals.

Example catalyst memory:

- April 9, 2025, 9:37 AM ET: Trump posted "THIS IS A GREAT TIME TO BUY!!! DJT" on Truth Social before a tariff-pause announcement. DJT was the only ticker in that explicit buy-style post.
- April 9, 2025, 9:33 AM ET: Trump posted a broad confidence message minutes before the buy-style post.
- Watchlist context from public Trump-related mentions includes DJT, INTC, AAPL, JPM, NVDA, BA, TSLA, MSFT, DELL, HPQ, and AMZN.

These are political/catalyst notes only. They do not override risk gates, liquidity, or price confirmation.

## Business Ecosystem

Pickaxe Capital is the trading and intelligence center. The broader business map can include TTT as the main brand, outsourced clothing/goods, websites, apps, import/export, coffee shop design, consumer goods, and multiple revenue streams.

Business intelligence should sit beside market intelligence so opportunities can compound across trading, products, websites, and physical ventures.

## Engineering Philosophy

Follow these rules strictly:

- Think before coding.
- Simplicity first.
- Surgical changes only.
- Goal-driven execution.
- Do not overengineer.
- Do not refactor unrelated files.
- Prefer mock data first when real data is not ready.
- Keep every feature easy to replace with real data later.

Success means the site works, the UI feels alive, and no unrelated behavior breaks.
